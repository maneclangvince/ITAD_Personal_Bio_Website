The Goals That i Want to Achieve

Becoming a Fullstack Software Engineer

Although I did some projects like a Database Management System during my Senior High School years, I still need to improve to become a Fullstack Software Engineer. I want to be like Bryl Lim and Jed Tan. I am inspired by them because they are Fullstack Software Engineers known in the TikTok community. I still have a long path to go and I make mistakes from time to time. But giving up is not an option when you want to become a Fullstack Software Engineer. That is why I need to continuously learn and stay consistent, especially since technology keeps evolving, and I should evolve as well.

Becoming Jack Of All Trades

The reason why I want to be a Jack Of All Trades is because I just saw this guy from YouTube and I'm inspired by him, his name is Mike Shake. He makes content about learning new skills that interest him, and he wants to try if he can do it or not. He is also an optimistic guy who doesn't give up on what he is learning. I want to be like that as well, to learn something new, gain new perspectives, and utilize it for my own good.

Getting Rich in Young Adult Years

I want to be rich in my late 20's because I want to financially help my family, treat my parents well as they helped me through my childhood, teenage, and college years, and even when I got sick. I also want to care for myself, buy things I want like a motorcycle, have my own condo and work in BGC, buy the clothes I want, have a gym room, and buy a PC with great performance.

Travelling to my Dream Places

When I get rich in my late 20's, the thing I want the most is travelling all around the world with my parents. This is not just my goal, but also my parents' goal. The only one who has travelled the world is my father because he is a seaman. But it's our dream because we think that if we travel the world together, we are finally free from our struggles and challenges.
